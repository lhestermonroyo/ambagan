Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Ambagan ("the App", "we", "us") is an expense-splitting app for groups, built by an independent developer based in the Philippines. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how it's handled.

1. Information We Collect

Data Why we collect it
Email / phone number Account creation and sign-in
Name Displaying your identity to group members
Profile photo Optional avatar shown to group members
Group & expense data Core app functionality — splitting and tracking shared expenses
Proof-of-payment photos Optional receipts/screenshots you attach to an expense
Push notification token Sending you expense and settlement notifications
Subscription status Unlocking Pro features you've paid for

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To let you create groups, add expenses, and split costs with others
  • To send you push notifications about activity relevant to you
  • To process and verify Pro subscription purchases

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers to operate the App. Each processes data only as needed to provide their service to us:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription purchase management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing for subscriptions
  • Expo — delivering push notifications

4. Data Sharing

Your name, photo, and expense activity are visible to other members of the groups you join — this is core to how the App works. We do not share your data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email us at lhester.monroyo.dev@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Ambagan is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email lhester.monroyo.dev@gmail.com.
