About Ambagan

"Ambag" is a Filipino word for contribution — to mag-ambag is to chip in. That's exactly what this app is built around: making it easy for groups of friends, family, or barkada to chip in fairly and keep track of who owes who.

Ambagan is built and maintained by an independent developer in the Philippines, designed to remove the awkward math and spreadsheets from splitting bills with the people you actually want to spend time with.
